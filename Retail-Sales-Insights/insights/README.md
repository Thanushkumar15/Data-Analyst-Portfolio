
# Retail Sales Insights - India

## Key Findings

1. Top Revenue Cities: Chennai, Kolkata, Mumbai
2. Top Profit Cities: Chennai, Kolkata, Ahmedabad
3. Top Revenue Category: Fashion with total revenue 17,554,064.68
4. Highest Profit Margin Category: Fashion with avg margin 15.29%
5. Month with highest revenue (combined across years): 12
6. Year with highest total revenue: 2024 with revenue 33,458,161.56
7. Discounts slightly increase revenue for Fashion category.
