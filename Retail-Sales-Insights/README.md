# Retail Sales Insights - India

## Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data across major Indian cities, providing actionable insights on revenue, profit, and product performance.

## Dataset
- **File:** `data/retail_sales_india.csv`
- Columns: Date, Invoice ID, Store City, Product Category, Product Name, Quantity Sold, Unit Price, Discount %, Revenue, Profit
- ~3,000 rows covering multiple cities and categories

## Key Analyses
- Total revenue and profit per city
- Revenue per product category
- Average profit margin per category
- Yearly revenue trends
- Top performing products
