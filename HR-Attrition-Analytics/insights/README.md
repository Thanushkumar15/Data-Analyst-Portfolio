
# HR Attrition Insights - India

## Key Findings

1. **Total Attrition:** 
   - Yes: 1547 employees
   - No: 1453 employees

2. **Department Insights:**  
   - Highest attrition: Research & Development

3. **Job Role Insights:**  
   - Highest attrition: Sales Executive

4. **Age & Experience:**  
   - Median age of leaving employees: 41.0 years  
   - Median years at company for leaving employees: 8.0 years

5. **OverTime Impact:**  
   - Employees working overtime leaving: 778 employees

6. **Gender Insights:**  
   - Attrition by gender: {'Female': 784, 'Male': 763}

7. **Actionable Insight for HR:**  
   - Focus retention strategies on high-attrition departments, roles, and employees with overtime and mid-range experience.
